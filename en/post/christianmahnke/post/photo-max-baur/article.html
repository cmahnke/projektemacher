Max Baur was a German photographer; his work is associated with New Objectivity (German). He completed an apprenticeship as a photographer in Eisenach from 1916 to 1919 and became particularly well known for his precise architectural and advertising photographs.

The image is a silver gelatine print and depicts cherry blossoms, presumably from a Prunus cerasus.
