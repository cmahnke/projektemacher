New biography of Franz Robert Fritz Neumann (1897-1984) aka Ric available

After the identity of Fritz Neumann and thus Ric could be clarified in October, Mark Strong from Meibohm Fine Arts compiled, condensed and published on AskArt on the basis of the findings from my blog.

Without a paid membership, the full biography can be viewed every Friday, otherwise it is also available for free at Meibohm Fine Arts.

Many thanks to Mark for his work!
