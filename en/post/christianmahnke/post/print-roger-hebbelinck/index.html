Lithograph by von Roger Hebbelinck (1912-1987)

I have found a lithograph by Roger Hebbelinck (1912–1987)…

Print
Signature

Roger Hebbelinck was primarily a painter and graphic artist who was born in Brussels in 1912 and trained in painting and etching at the local Academie des Beaux Arts. In 1932, he founded his own copperplate printing studio. Artistically, he specialised primarily in depicting historical cityscapes.

The name of the motif is ‘De oude brug’ (de Peerdenbrug te Brugge) and shows the Peerden Bridge in Bruges (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruges). Further views of the bridge can be found at Wikimedia Commons (https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Peerdenbrug_(Brugge)).
