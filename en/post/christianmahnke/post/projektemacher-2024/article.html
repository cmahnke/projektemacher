Projektemacher 2024

At the end of 2024, here is the annual retrospect for Projektemacher.org. Over 270 articles were published over the course of the year (the most active year to date).

Some of the highlights are

  • 70s wallpaper - which was then also published as first HDR IIIF image.
    • And the corresponding Lightning Talk at the IIIF Online Meeting 2024
  • Fritz Neumann (1928-2014) is not Ric! - But the right Fritz Neumann (1897-1984) was finally found
  • On the ruthless nature of metropolitan day-trippers - the first entry with text to speech
  • die neue linie - March 1930 - The Bauhaus lifestyle magazine.
  • “Reihenhaus-Fassaden Geschäfts- und Wohnhäuser aus alter und neuer Zeit” - Many examples of avant-garde architecture from 1929
  • Licht 2024 - My projects on light and images, like:
    • Contrast enhancement for UV images using HDR
    • Ultraviolet Photogrammetry

New pages

  • Vintage Reality - Historical stereo images

New functions

  • Clothes hangers are now displayed on a map
  • Children’s books can now be exported as PDFs
  • Ric - An unknown artist is now available in a German translation
  • Web archiving - Some blogs now send their links to the Internet Archive. The blogs themselves will be archived at the DNB in the future.
  • IIIF collections for blog content (View in Theseus)
  • 911TV is now available as a desktop application, e.g. for a physical installation.

Events

  • After my Christmas cards have been very popular in recent years, there was a craft workshop this year.
