Exhibition "Wilma Bräuner"

From December 6, the exhibition “Spurensuche im Sylter Archiv - Zum Leben und Werk von Wilma Bräuner (1891 - 1985)”, on which Evelyn Kraßmann and I worked together with a team, will take place in the city gallery “Alte Post”, Stephanstraße 4, Sylt / OT Westerland. As part of the preparations for the exhibition we have created a Wikipedia entry, the website is still under construction…

The vernissage will take place on Monday, December 5, 2022 at 6 pm in the Stadtgalerie.
